:MOCVD stands for Metal-Organic Chemical Vapour Deposition
>GRINSCH (Graded Index Confinement Heterostructure)
>World wide maker,  two main metal-organic chemical vapor deposition system makers Aixtron and Veeco.
Major MOCVD vendors are AIXTRON, Thomas Swan, VEECO (Emcore), Nippon-Sanso
>System overview. The whole system contains five parts.
	- gas blending unit
	- control unit
	- reactor with heated susceptor
	- vaccum system
	- scrubbing system
